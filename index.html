// about

Models worth zooming in on.

I'm a 3D modeler who also codes. I model and sculpt assets, then write the scripts and tools that make them faster to build and easier to ship.

Every asset I deliver is built to a standard: clean, even topology, sensible UVs, and materials that hold up under an extreme close-up. Nothing leaves the viewport unless it looks right.

I work mostly in Blender, with Unreal, Unity, and Roblox Studio when a project needs it. When something needs a script or a small tool, I write that too.
