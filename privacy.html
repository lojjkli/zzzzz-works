ZGuard - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ZGuard (“the Bot”), developed by ZzzZz Works, handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

ZGuard may collect limited data, such as:

  • Discord user IDs, guild IDs, and message content (only when moderation features are triggered).
  • Server configuration data (e.g., roles, permissions, prefixes).

2. How We Use Data

Data is used solely for providing bot functionality, including moderation, security features, and logging.

3. Data Retention

All stored data is retained only as long as needed for bot operation. Upon bot removal from a server, related configuration data may be automatically deleted.

4. Sharing of Data

ZGuard does not share or sell your data to third parties. Data may be shared only if required by Discord’s API rules or applicable law.

5. Your Rights

You may request data removal for your Discord server by contacting the ZzzZz Works team through the official Discord server.

6. Updates to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. The date of the latest version will be indicated below.
